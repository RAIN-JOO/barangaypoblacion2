Our Services

Health Services

Our Barangay Health Center provides basic health care, immunizations, first aid, and family planning services for all residents.

Peace and Order

Barangay Tanods help maintain peace and order. Our Barangay make sure that everyone is safe at night.

Education Services

Our Barangay early childhood education at the Barangay Daycare Center and vocational training for skills development and livelihood opportunities. Our Barangay also offers free school supplies.

Social Services

Our Barangay provide assistance to senior citizens, PWDs, and offer disaster relief programs. We also handle civil registry services like issuing Barangay Certificates.
